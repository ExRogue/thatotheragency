Meta Ads for London DTC Brands: Creative Testing Framework

Published February 18, 2026

Most Meta accounts fail because they test too many variables at once. Better results come from a simple, repeated testing structure with clear pass/fail criteria.

Week structure

  • Week 1: test 3 hooks against one offer
  • Week 2: scale top hook with 3 new body angles
  • Week 3: test new creative formats (UGC, demo, founder-led)
  • Week 4: consolidate winners and shift budget

What to measure

Measure hold-rate, thumb-stop rate, CTR, and post-click conversion rate. Winning creatives need both attention and conversion, not one without the other.

Common London DTC pitfall

Brands often over-invest in polished content and under-invest in angle variety. Volume of angle testing usually beats production polish early on.
