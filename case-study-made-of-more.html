Case study · Made Of More

Improving lead quality through offer clarity and paid media structure

Made Of More needed stronger conversion consistency across paid channels. We focused on messaging hierarchy, campaign architecture, and landing-page relevance.

B2B

Service-led engagement model

12 weeks

Initial implementation phase

Weekly

Optimisation and reporting cycle

4 streams

Strategy, media, creative, CRO

Challenge

High spend variability and inconsistent lead quality made planning difficult for the commercial team.

  • Offer messaging was too broad
  • Campaign segmentation diluted intent signals
  • Landing experience did not match ad promise

Approach

We rebuilt the demand pathway so audience intent, ad message, and landing conversion were aligned.

  • Refined proposition and core claims
  • Restructured campaigns by intent and objective
  • Updated conversion pages and qualification flow

Outcome

The programme gave the team clearer conversion diagnostics and more stable paid media decision-making. Reporting shifted from vanity metrics to commercially useful lead quality indicators.

  • Higher proportion of sales-relevant leads
  • Better visibility into campaign-level quality drivers
  • Faster weekly decisions across channel and creative
